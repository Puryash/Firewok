Our Story

Here at Burger Site, we're passionate about burgers. Our founder, Waqar, grew up in a small town where there was only one burger. He dreamed of creating a restaurant that offered a wide variety of burgers.

After years of perfecting his recipes and finding the right team, Burger Site was born. Today, we're proud to serve our community with the best burgers around.
